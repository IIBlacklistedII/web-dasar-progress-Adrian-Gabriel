Visas & Immigration

"Navigating your entry requirements is the first essential step toward exploring the deep historical roots of Syria."

All international travelers wishing to visit Syria are required to obtain a valid entry visa prior to arrival. To secure a tourist visa, applicants must submit a completed application form, a passport with at least six months of validity beyond the intended stay, passport-sized photographs, and proof of a booked travel itinerary or a security clearance processed via an authorized local tour operator. Please note that passports must not contain any entry or exit stamps from Israel, as this will result in an immediate denial of entry.

The Application Process ▼

Independent tourist visa applications can be requested directly at your nearest Syrian Embassy or Consulate. Alternatively, the most common route is acquiring a Security Clearance through an approved Syrian travel agency, which grants you a visa-on-arrival at land borders or the airport. Processing times generally range from two to several weeks depending on your nationality.

Visa Fees & Border Customs ▼

Visa issuance fees vary substantially based on the applicant's country of citizenship and are typically paid in cash (USD or Euros) directly at the border checkpoint. Upon clearing passport control, immigration officers will issue an entry stamp specifying your allowed duration of stay. Always double-check current bilateral regulations before attempting a crossing.
