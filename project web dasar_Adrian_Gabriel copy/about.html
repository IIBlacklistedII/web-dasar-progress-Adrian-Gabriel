About Syria Tourism Board

The Syria Tourism Board is the official organization responsible for promoting and developing tourism in Syria. It works to showcase the rich cultural heritage, historical sites, and natural beauty of the country to attract visitors from around the world.

This website is a project created as part of our web development course. The content and design are intended to provide an informative and engaging experience for users interested in learning about Syria's tourism offerings. While simultaneously serving as a practical application of our web development skills, we hope it also inspires curiosity and appreciation for Syria's unique attractions and cultural heritage.
