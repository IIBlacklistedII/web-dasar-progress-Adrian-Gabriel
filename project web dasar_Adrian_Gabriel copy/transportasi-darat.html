GETTING AROUND

Ground Transportation Guide & Fares in Syria

Syria's ground transportation system is a mix of public and private options, with buses, minibuses (known as "mikrobus"), and taxis being the most common choices for travelers.

Ground Transportation

Exploring Syria by land offers breathtaking scenery, ranging from historic urban areas to stunning desert roads. Syria’s land-based public transportation system relies on a reliable network of intercity buses and very affordable local transportation.

For travelers, understanding the options for ground transportation and their estimated fares is essential for planning daily travel while in major cities such as Damascus, Aleppo, or Latakia.

💡 Important Tips for Overland Travel

Always have cash on hand in the local currency (Syrian Pound / SYP) in small denominations, as most public transportation services, such as Serfis, do not accept digital payments or credit cards. For intercity travel by Pullman bus, it is recommended that you purchase your ticket one day before departure at the main terminal to ensure seat availability.
